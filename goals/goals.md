## Scoring Guidelines

- **Beginner tasks**: 10-15 points
- **Intermediate tasks**: 20-30 points  
- **Advanced tasks**: 35-45 points
- **Bonus challenges**: 25-50 points

**Team Formation**: Consider teams of 2-3 people to encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing.

**Time Limit**: 4-6 hours for the hackathon, with periodic check-ins.

**Prizes**: 
- Highest total score
- Most creative use of Copilot
- Best refactoring improvement
- Most comprehensive test coverage added
